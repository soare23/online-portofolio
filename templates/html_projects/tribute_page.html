As many other great fotball players, he also had a very good carrer as a coach. He is currently a coach for the team he owns - Viitorul Constanta- where he is focusing on developing young kids and helping them achive their dream of becoming world class fotball players.

Short brief of his Footbal Player Career:

  • 1982–1983 Farul Constanta
  • 1983–1987 Sportul Stundentesc
  • 1987–1990 Steaua Bucuresti
  • 1990–1992 Real Madrid
  • 1992–1994 Brescia
  • 1994–1996 Barcelona
  • 1996–2001 Galatasaray

And a short brief of his Coaching Career:

  • 2001 Romania
  • 2003–2004 Buraspor
  • 2004–2005 Galatasaray
  • 2005–2006 Politehnica Timișoara
  • 2007 Steaua București
  • 2010–2011 Galatasaray
  • 2014- Viitorul Constanța
